Research

  • Stookey Internship (Corning, Inc., 2012)

  • Undergraduate Research (Coe College and University of Iowa, 2009-2011)
    • Experimented with different chemical combinations in order to develop a scintillator for the Compact Muon Solenoid (CMS) detector with certain properties that would allow the material to survive in the harsh radiation environment in the LHC experiment.
    • Synthesized and studied lithium and cesium borovanadate glasses using characterization techniques such as nuclear megnetic resonance (NMR), differential scanning calorimetry, and electron spin resonance.
    • Rapid- and slow-cooled two samples of 10Boron glass to send to University of Warwick, England for NMR studies on the samplesâ€™ internal structures due to differing cooling rates.
    • Made, annealed, and polished cesium borate glass samples for experiments to improve preliminary atomic clocks at MIT Draper Laboratory.
  • Mathematics Game Theory Research (Coe College, 2010-2013)
    • Explored the regularities in the Grundy sequence for linear color cram with the goal of proving that not all octal games are periodic.
